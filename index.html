About tyra d

Rising from the Southside of Chicago, Tyra D is a singer/songwriter, actress, and model. From pirouettes to the big stage, Tyra D has been in Performing Arts since the age of four. Marrying her love of performance, she found in ballet and modeling classes with her passion for singing attained by performing in the school choir since grade school, Tyra D recognized her calling!

She began creating her own opportunities to perform by writing movie scripts as a young child. Tyra D has audacious, yet graceful stage presence. Addicted to the limelight, she went on to AMDA College and Conservatory of Performing Arts in Los Angeles, CA, naturally, majoring in Performing Arts with a concentration in Musical Theatre. Tyra D is represented by Lily’s Talent Agency for Chicago based acting and modeling.

In 2016, Tyra D released her first single “Afraid to Love” discussing her apprehensiveness of entering a new relationship. This single built a buzz for the R&B singer who was featured in Elevator, True Star and Cliché Magazines. She was also interviewed by Power 92 radio personality, Chi-Blizz. She plans to follow in the footsteps of icons like Chaka Khan and Bruno Mars. With the October 2019 release of her single “No Reply” as well as her upcoming EP that is in the works. Tyra D assures she is well on her way. Tyra D is an artist to keep an eye out for as she breaks into the entertainment industry.
